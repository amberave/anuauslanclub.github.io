## Sign Vocabulary

### [Auslan Signbank (of course!)](https://auslan.org.au/)

### [Find Sign](https://find.auslan.fyi/)

Search engine for Signbank and other sites/pages that upload Auslan signs.

Tip: If there's a less common sign or a newer sign, it may not have been uploaded to Signbank but may be discoverable on Find Sign.

## Organizations

### [Deaf Connect](https://deafconnect.org.au/)

Deaf Connect (formerly The Deaf Society) is the official organization in the ACT that offers accredited Auslan classes for Auslan I & II, as well as Certificates II-IV and Diploma of Auslan.

## Narratives

### [Auslan Storybooks](http://www.auslanstorybooks.com/the-storyteller.html)

Trudy Fraser has uploaded a number of stories told in Auslan by herself and other d/Deaf storytellers[^1]

## History

### [DeafTVAussie on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@deaftvaussie)

An active channel on YouTube that uploads videos on d/Deaf history and culture.

### [Online Biographies of Deaf People in NSW](http://deafinnsw.com/)

The site Deaf in NSW hosts a record of over one hundred deaf Australians. Each dedicated page includes a short biography and a bibliography. This can be a good resource when researching deaf historical figures in Australia.
